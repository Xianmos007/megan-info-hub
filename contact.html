City Council Meetings

The Titusville City Council meets regularly to discuss and vote on matters affecting our city. All meetings are open to the public, and I encourage residents to attend and participate.

Regular Meeting Schedule

1st and 3rd Tuesday of each month at 7:00 PM
Titusville City Hall Council Chambers
555 S. Washington Ave
Titusville, FL 32796

Public Comment

Residents are invited to speak during the public comment portion of each meeting. If you'd like to address the Council, please arrive early to sign up for public comment.
