About Megan Moscoso

Megan Moscoso, Titusville City Council Member

As a dedicated member of the Titusville City Council, I am committed to serving our community with integrity, transparency, and a forward-thinking approach. My journey in public service began with a simple desire to make a positive difference in the place I call home.

Born and raised in Titusville, I have deep roots in our community. After graduating from Titusville High School, I earned my degree in Public Administration from the University of Florida. My professional background includes experience in community development, project management, and public outreach.

I believe that local government should be accessible to all residents and that every voice deserves to be heard. My approach to governance is collaborative, inclusive, and focused on finding practical solutions to the challenges we face as a growing city.

Vision & Values

Community First

I believe in putting the needs of our community first. Every decision I make is guided by what will benefit the residents of Titusville, both now and in the future.

Fiscal Responsibility

I am committed to responsible stewardship of taxpayer dollars, ensuring that public funds are used efficiently and effectively to provide essential services and improve our quality of life.

Sustainable Growth

I support thoughtful development that balances economic growth with environmental protection and preserves the unique character of our city for generations to come.

Transparent Governance

I believe in open, honest communication with constituents. I am committed to making government processes more transparent and ensuring that residents have access to the information they need.
